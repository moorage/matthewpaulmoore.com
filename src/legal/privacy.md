  <h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
  <p class="lede"><strong>Swift Markdown Viewer</strong> is a local-first app for viewing Markdown files on macOS, iPhone, and iPad.</p>

We do not intentionally collect, sell, or share personal information through the app itself.

The app processes files and folders that you choose to open. Those files are used on-device to render their contents for you. If the app references local media inside the opened folder, that media is also processed on-device for display.

We do not require account creation, and we do not currently use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking technologies in the app.

If you contact support directly, we may receive the information you choose to include in your message, such as your email address and the contents of your request. We use that information only to respond to your support inquiry.

If the app behavior changes in the future to add analytics, crash reporting, accounts, cloud sync, or any other networked feature, this policy should be updated before that version is released.
